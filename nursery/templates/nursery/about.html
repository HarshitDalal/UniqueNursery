What We Do

We create green spaces, both indoors and outdoors. We are here to make the concrete jungle, we call home, a greener and more sustainable space through customized gardening solutions. Through our Own Grown Farms initiative we convert open urban spaces into food gardens to lower food miles and promote community gardening values.

How We Do It

We are driven by our passion to promote sustainability as a feasible way of life. We are devoted to cultivating a community of gardeners who are as passionate about gardening as we are and hand holding those who are not yet fully convinced, to dive deep into the green world with us.

Our Mission

We have big dreams - for everyone. Fully sustainable food farms on every inch of empty unused urban real estate, pan India dispatch centres and nurseries that deliver plants to every home in every pin code, and a family of customers who are more family than customer. We aim to make growing our own food a norm to reduce the carbon footprint we generate and in the process neutralize environmental carbon percentage further.
